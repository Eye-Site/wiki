About EyeSite

EyeSite is an accessibility tool designed to make the web more user-friendly. With a range of customizable features, you can adjust the visual presentation of websites to meet your needs.

What is EyeSite

This extension empowers users to take matters into their own hands, providing the control and flexibility to tailor the online experience to their preferences. EyeSite restores autonomy to people with disabilities, ensuring they no longer have to depend solely on website creators to produce accessible content. By enabling users to personalize web accessibility, EyeSite promotes independence and makes the internet a more inclusive space for everyone.
